User Guide

Hover over the specific country to view its trade data with Portugal

Use the slider to specify a trade amount range to filter trade partners within that range

The range slider is bounded by the trade amount of the 11th largest trade partner out of all the countries

Bar chart displays the top 10 largest trade partners within the trade amount range user specifies

Project Inspiration

Portugal has taken a great role in global trade since the Age of Discovery.

The purpose of this project is to visualize Portugal's Geoeconomic influence in the world.
